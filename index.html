Modular System

Our modular system makes customizations tailored to your needs simple.

These planters can be extened in 4' or 8' sections in 90 degree orientations. So this means you can have a rectangle 4' by 8' planter. You could extent this by 4' and create an L shaped planter. Extend this more and create a U shaped planter with a walkway.
